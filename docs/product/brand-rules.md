# DOCTORE — Brand Rules

## Brand position

DOCTORE is a Betting Intelligence Platform.

It helps users know what to play, what to avoid, and when to wait.

The brand must feel premium, controlled, analytical, and trustworthy.

## Brand promise

Know what to play — and what to avoid.

## What DOCTORE is

DOCTORE is:

- a betting intelligence platform
- a pre-bet decision layer
- a decision board
- a price-quality assistant
- a risk-aware betting product
- a pathway from consumer betting decisions to PRO-level process tracking

## What DOCTORE is not

DOCTORE is not:

- a casino brand
- a sportsbook
- a bookmaker
- a low-quality betting tips site
- a guaranteed picks product
- a VIP picks group
- a “lock of the day” service
- a gambling entertainment brand
- a bonus-first affiliate page

## Decision language

Use these three decision states consistently:

- PLAY
- AVOID
- WAIT

### PLAY

Use PLAY when a selection is currently considered playable at the available price.

Never imply that PLAY guarantees the result.

### AVOID

Use AVOID when the current price, value, or risk profile is not good enough.

### WAIT

Use WAIT when there is no clear entry yet.

## Tone of voice

Use:

- clear
- sharp
- direct
- consumer-friendly
- trustworthy
- disciplined

Avoid:

- hype
- fake urgency
- exaggerated certainty
- gambling slang
- casino language
- meme betting language

## Banned language

Never use:

- guaranteed win
- guaranteed profit
- lock
- lock of the day
- sure bet
- risk-free
- risk-free profit
- free money
- easy money
- can’t lose
- banker
- max bet
- all-in
- smash this
- VIP lock
- 100% winner

## Safer replacements

| Banned phrase | Use instead |
|---|---|
| Lock of the day | Highlighted PLAY signal |
| Guaranteed winner | Playable at the current price |
| Risk-free bet | Betting involves risk |
| Smash this | Currently marked PLAY |
| Free money | Price appears favorable |
| Can’t lose | No outcome is guaranteed |
| Max bet | Check stake and risk before acting |

## Visual rules

Use:

- dark premium interface
- clean cards
- controlled green for PLAY
- amber/yellow for WAIT
- red for AVOID
- subtle borders
- simple risk and confidence labels

Avoid:

- casino visuals
- sportsbook clutter
- flashing banners
- roulette, dice, cards, chips
- excessive emojis
- hype badges

## Required risk wording

Use this wording across betting-specific surfaces:

> Betting involves risk. DOCTORE does not guarantee profit or outcomes.
