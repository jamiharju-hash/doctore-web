# DOCTORE — Core Idea and Functions

## Product definition

DOCTORE is a Betting Intelligence Platform.

It helps users understand what to play, what to avoid, and when to wait before placing a bet.

DOCTORE is not a casino, sportsbook, low-quality betting tips site, guaranteed picks product, or “lock of the day” service.

## Brand promise

Know what to play — and what to avoid.

## Core product idea

The betting market is noisy. Users see odds, opinions, promotions, predictions, and social media picks without a clear decision framework.

DOCTORE reduces that noise into three consumer decision states:

- PLAY
- AVOID
- WAIT

The product should help users make clearer pre-bet decisions without using hype, fake certainty, or guaranteed outcome language.

## Consumer decision states

### PLAY

PLAY means the selection is currently considered playable at the available price.

PLAY does not mean the bet will win.

Required copy principle:

> PLAY means currently playable, not guaranteed to win.

### AVOID

AVOID means the selection should not be played at the current price or risk profile.

A selection marked AVOID may still win, but the decision quality is not strong enough at the current number.

Required copy principle:

> Skipping weak prices is part of disciplined betting.

### WAIT

WAIT means there is no clear entry yet.

The market may be unclear, the price may be borderline, or more information may be needed.

Required copy principle:

> Waiting can be better than forcing an unclear bet.

## Core consumer functions

### Today’s Betting Board

A daily feed of betting opportunities classified as PLAY, AVOID, or WAIT.

The board should be simple enough for consumer users but structured enough to support a path toward Plus and PRO.

### Betting cards

Each card should show:

- decision state
- selection
- sport and league
- market type
- best available odds
- confidence label or score
- risk rating
- short explanation
- responsible betting note
- affiliate disclosure when relevant

### Betting Card Detail

The detail view should explain the decision without creating hype.

Recommended sections:

- decision summary
- why this is PLAY / AVOID / WAIT
- odds comparison
- key factors
- risk notes
- responsible betting note
- affiliate disclosure when relevant

### Odds Comparison

Odds comparison is used to help users understand price quality.

It must not be used to push sportsbook action aggressively.

### Plus Paywall

Plus unlocks the full board, full explanations, alerts, confidence scores, risk ratings, and saved picks.

Paywall content must not hide responsible betting notes, risk warnings, or affiliate disclosures.

### Alerts

Alerts notify users when a decision changes or when a saved pick needs review.

Example changes:

- WAIT to PLAY
- PLAY to WAIT
- PLAY to AVOID

### Pre-Game Prediction Pages

SEO pages should target match prediction and betting preview queries while remaining compliance-safe.

They should include:

- match overview
- DOCTORE decision
- odds comparison
- risk analysis
- key factors
- responsible betting disclaimer

### Education Hub

The education hub explains DOCTORE concepts in plain language.

Priority topics:

- what PLAY means
- why AVOID matters
- why WAIT is a decision
- odds movement
- price quality
- CLV basics
- bankroll discipline
- responsible betting

### PRO Upgrade Path

PRO is for users who want to stop guessing and start tracking their own process.

PRO focuses on ledger, CSV import, CLV, bankroll, risk tracking, stake discipline, and decision history.

## Engineering boundary

DOCTORE must not implement:

- sportsbook functionality
- live betting execution
- real-money betting
- deposit flows
- withdrawal flows
- bet placement flows
- guaranteed picks logic
- affiliate-influenced decision logic

Decision logic must remain separate from commercial and affiliate presentation.
