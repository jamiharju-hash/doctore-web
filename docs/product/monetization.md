# DOCTORE — Monetization

## Monetization principle

DOCTORE monetizes through subscriptions and affiliate partnerships while keeping decision integrity independent from commercial incentives.

Commercial logic must never influence PLAY, AVOID, or WAIT.

## Product tiers

### Free

Free is the entry tier.

Includes:

- limited daily betting board
- selected PLAY / AVOID / WAIT cards
- limited explanations
- basic education content

Purpose:

- introduce the DOCTORE decision system
- convert users into email, Telegram, and Plus users

### Plus

Plus is the paid consumer tier.

Includes:

- full daily betting board
- complete card explanations
- alerts
- confidence scores
- risk ratings
- saved picks

Purpose:

- help active consumer users review the full board before acting
- increase trust through explanations and risk notes

### PRO

PRO is the process tracking tier.

Includes:

- ledger
- CSV import
- CLV tracking
- bankroll management
- risk checks
- stake discipline
- decision history

Purpose:

- help users stop guessing and start tracking their own process

### SHARP

SHARP is the advanced user tier.

Includes:

- API integrations
- exposure control
- advanced CLV
- sharper market comparison
- operational risk views

Purpose:

- support advanced users who need deeper control and data integration

### INFRA

INFRA is custom infrastructure for teams.

Includes:

- custom integrations
- data pipelines
- team dashboards
- reporting infrastructure
- custom workflows

Purpose:

- serve teams, syndicates, and professional workflows

## Affiliate monetization

Affiliate links may be used on:

- betting card CTAs
- odds comparison tables
- match preview pages
- daily board placements
- email and Telegram alerts

Affiliate placement must always be disclosed.

## Commercial separation rule

Affiliate metadata must be stored and rendered separately from decision records.

Decision records may contain:

- decision state
- confidence score
- risk rating
- price quality
- market context
- explanation

Affiliate records may contain:

- bookmaker name
- affiliate URL
- tracking parameters
- jurisdiction availability
- disclosure requirement

## Forbidden monetization behavior

Never allow:

- affiliate payout to change decision state
- partner sportsbook to force PLAY status
- sportsbook ranking to override best available price
- AVOID cards to aggressively push betting action
- paywall to hide responsible betting warnings
- paywall to hide affiliate disclosures
