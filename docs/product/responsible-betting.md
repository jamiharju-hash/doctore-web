# DOCTORE — Responsible Betting

## Core rule

Betting involves risk. DOCTORE does not guarantee profit, winning bets, or outcomes.

This statement must be visible on betting-specific surfaces.

## Product responsibility

DOCTORE provides betting intelligence and decision support.

It does not remove uncertainty from betting.

The product must not encourage reckless betting, emotional staking, loss chasing, or overconfidence.

## Required responsible betting principles

DOCTORE should remind users to:

- only stake what they can afford to lose
- avoid chasing losses
- avoid increasing stakes emotionally
- use limits where available
- take breaks when decision quality drops
- skip unclear markets
- treat PLAY as a decision state, not a guarantee

## Required microcopy

### Universal note

> Betting involves risk. DOCTORE does not guarantee profit or outcomes.

### PLAY note

> PLAY means currently playable, not guaranteed to win.

### AVOID note

> Skipping weak prices is part of disciplined betting.

### WAIT note

> Waiting can be better than forcing an unclear bet.

### CTA-adjacent note

> Only stake what you can afford to lose.

## Decision state safety

### PLAY

PLAY must never imply certainty.

Allowed:

- Currently playable at the listed price.
- Price remains within the playable range.
- Decision clarity is strong.

Forbidden:

- Guaranteed winner.
- Lock.
- Can’t lose.
- Safe bet.

### AVOID

AVOID should normalize skipping.

Allowed:

- Current price is not attractive enough.
- Risk profile is elevated.
- No bet at this number.

Forbidden:

- This team cannot win.
- Impossible outcome.

### WAIT

WAIT should be framed as a valid decision.

Allowed:

- No clear entry yet.
- Market is still moving.
- Wait for better price or more information.

Forbidden:

- Bet before it disappears.
- Act now.
- Last chance.

## Paywall responsibility

Responsible betting notes must never be hidden behind a paywall.

Free users must still see:

- betting risk reminder
- no-guarantee language
- affiliate disclosure when relevant
- state-specific risk notes when a card is visible

## Alerts responsibility

Alerts must not create panic or urgency.

Use:

- Decision changed: WAIT to PLAY.
- Price moved into playable range.
- Review before acting.

Avoid:

- Bet now.
- Last chance.
- Urgent winner.
- Don’t miss this.

## PRO responsibility

PRO should reinforce discipline through:

- bankroll tracking
- stake discipline
- risk checks
- decision history
- CLV education

PRO must not imply that tracking guarantees profit.

## Internal QA checklist

Before release, check that:

- no banned language appears in UI copy
- PLAY cards include risk-safe copy
- affiliate CTAs include disclosure
- paywall does not hide risk warnings
- alerts do not create fake urgency
- sportsbook execution is not implemented
- real-money betting flows are not implemented
